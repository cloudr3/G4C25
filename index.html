PITCH

In Celestara: Echoes of the Sky, a young boy named Eryx must fight to restore a fractured world of floating islands, torn apart by warlord Lord Varyn's ruthless reign. After a fateful fall from the islands of Celestara, Eryx stumbles upon a new world. He uncovers powerful resources that could save Celestara - but Varyn still hunts for control, even within this new world. Use ancient magic and rare minerals to battle through treacherous lands, solve mysteries, and rebuild peace. With dynamic exploration, survival mechanics, and a rich story, Celestara challenges you to navigate political strife and shape the future of two worlds.

THEME

In Celestara, players explore a fractured world torn apart by ancient chains of power. Through Eryx's journey—unfolding via dialogue, lore, and atmospheric cutscenes—they witness the aftermath of conflict and the need for restoration. Floating islands scarred by magic reflect a world on the brink. Gameplay blends survival, exploration, and choice-driven paths where combat or diplomacy alter Celestara's fate. As players gather resources (that way, everyone back in the world of Celestara can survive and continue to rebuild all that was destroyed), relics and unlock lost abilities, every decision shapes the world's future, reinforcing a central theme: the struggle for balance, unity, and peace in the face of overwhelming division and a rising threat—Lord Varyn.

MY WHY

This game is very important to me, not only just because it is my first ever game I have made, but it was the first ever game where I was able to actually sit down and put ideas I had as a child into a huge screen! As a kid, I always had dreams of creating such interesting and vast stories for games I would like to create in the future. Many games (specifically Indie games) have made my ideas grow as years went on and I was inspired on what creating a story can really do! After seeing and playing so much of these games, all these different stories, all these different characters, have created such a huge love for game design and story telling in my heart. It is such a fun thing to do, and to finally implement just one of my ideas into a simple Roll A Ball game has brought me so much joy that you can't even imagine. I put so much effort into creating the environment in Unity, the enemy, etc! My ideas for world building, story telling, character designs, and even more, still has not stopped yet and I still want to create even more and more! That is why this game, and creating games is so important to me.
